Economic management is the foundation of long-term match success. Forcing buys too frequently will ruin your economy, while saving too often concedes map control and round momentum to the opposition.

| Economy Call | Cost Range | Tactical Objective |
|---|---|---|
| **Full Eco** | $0 - $1,500 | Save maximum resources to guarantee a full buy next round. |
| **Force Buy** | $2,000 - $3,200 | Purchase pistols and light armor to catch the opponent off-guard. |
| **Half Buy** | $3,000 - $3,800 | Maintain enough bank reserves to buy utility in subsequent rounds. |
| **Full Buy** | $4,500+ | Equip full armor, primary rifles (or AWPs), and complete utility sets. |

<Callout type="tip" title="Timeout Management">
