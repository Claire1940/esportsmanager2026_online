<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Player Development and Mental Health">

High-performance athletes are not robots. In **esports manager 2026 cs2**, managing the mental well-being of your roster is just as important as aim training. The game's **Talk Module** allows for deep interaction with players and staff. Understanding their individual motivations—whether it's fame, salary, or winning trophies—is key to long-term retention and performance.

**Weekly Training Priorities:**
- **Skill Growth**: Targeted drills for Aim, Utility, or Movement.
- **Team Building**: Activities designed to increase the "Chemistry" stat.
- **Mental Resilience**: Psychology sessions to boost the Mental attribute before major events.
- **Rest/Recovery**: Essential to prevent burnout and injury.

<Callout type="info" title="Development Tip">
Junior players develop faster but are more prone to Morale swings. Pair a veteran leader with a rookie to accelerate the younger player's growth while stabilizing their mental state.
</Callout>
