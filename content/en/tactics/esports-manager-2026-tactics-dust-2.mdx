<Section icon={Shield} title="CT-Side Defensive Setups">

Dust 2's CT-side is notorious for being difficult to hold if you lose mid control. A balanced setup usually involves two players holding A-long/short, one player monitoring mid from CT-spawn, and two players anchoring the B-site. However, you must adapt your setups based on your economy.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Early Round Utility">
    Deploy early smokes at B-tunnels and short-A to delay fast rushes and deny basic vision.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Mid Information Gathering">
    Position your primary AWPer or high-perception rifler at mid doors to spot crossing T-side players.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Cross-Fire Establishment">
    Set up cross-fire positions at A-long or deep inside B-site, ensuring players can trade kills effectively.
  </Step>
  <Steps num="4" title="Retake Coordination">
    In post-plant scenarios, wait for rotating teammates and execute retakes together using flashes rather than entering dry.
  </Steps>
</Steps>
