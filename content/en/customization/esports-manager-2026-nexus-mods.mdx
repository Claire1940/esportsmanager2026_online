<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate Game Directory">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Download and Extract">
    Download your chosen mod from Nexus Mods. Use a tool like 7-Zip or WinRAR to extract the files. Look for a folder named "Graphics" or "Database" within the extracted contents.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Apply Assets">
    Drag and drop the extracted folders into the game's root directory. If prompted to "Replace existing files," select yes. This is common for logo packs that overwrite generic placeholders.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Clear In-Game Cache">
    Launch the game and navigate to the Settings menu. Under the "Interface" tab, select "Reload Skin" or "Clear Cache." This forces the engine to recognize the new images and data entries.
