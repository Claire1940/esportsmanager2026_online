Economic management separates average managers from championship contenders. Playing on autopilot and selecting standard buys every round will destroy your long-term success. You must treat every eco, semi-buy, and force buy as a tactical weapon to manipulate the opponent's finances.

| Round Scenario | Financial Status | Recommended Action | Strategic Objective |
|---|---|---|---|
