<Section icon={Shield} title="Economic Mastery and In-Game Interventions">

Managing your team's finances is just as important as tactical positioning. In Esports Manager 2026, playing on autopilot with standard buys every round will ruin your economy. You must treat every eco, semi-buy, and force buy as a deliberate strategic tool.

| Buy Type | Cost Level | Risk Level | Strategic Purpose |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Full Eco** | Zero Spend | Low Risk | Save maximum cash for the next round |
| **Force Buy** | Medium Spend | High Risk | Break the opponent's economy and momentum |
| **Half Buy** | Low Spend | Medium Risk | Maintain utility while saving for a full buy |
| **Full Buy** | High Spend | Low Risk | Deploy optimal weaponry and full utility |

During live match simulations, do not treat the game as an automatic spectator mode. You must actively intervene when things go wrong. If your team loses two rounds in a row, call a tactical timeout immediately to reorganize your buy, adjust your setups, or settle player anxiety.

<Callout type="success" title="Economic Tip">
A well-timed force buy can completely flip the momentum of a match. If you successfully break the enemy team's economy, they will be forced into consecutive eco rounds, allowing you to seize control of the series.
</Callout>
