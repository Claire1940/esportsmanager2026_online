<Section icon={Shield} title="Roster Management: Contract Clauses & Financial Health">

Navigating the transfer market requires patience and strict financial discipline. Impulse buying expensive superstars will quickly drain your budget and limit your flexibility when the meta shifts.

### Smart Contract Negotiation
When signing **esports manager 2026 players**, look closely at performance bonuses, buyout clauses, and contract lengths. High performance bonuses might look attractive initially, but they can easily inflate your operational costs if the team starts winning consistently.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Identify Targets">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Negotiate Clauses">
    Keep base salaries manageable by offering sensible, milestone-based performance bonuses rather than flat high wages.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Utilize Loans">

  </Step>
